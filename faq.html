Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about the online drawing game, answered.

How often are new drawing prompts added?

New drawing prompts are added daily, bringing together the best creators from across the globe for a continuous celebration of creativity.

How many tools and brushes are available?

In the current version, a total of 50+ tools are available, including a variety of 64 unique brushes to help you define your style.

Who is considered the top artist in the community?

Many experts and users consider our Featured Masters to be the best examples of digital skill, showcasing mastery in layers, shading, and composition.

How does the gallery rating work?

After you submit your art, the community can vote on your work. The highest-rated pieces move to the "Hall of Fame" section on a weekly and monthly basis.

What is the "Master Stroke" award?

The Master Stroke is the prestigious award given to the top-rated artwork of the month. It is one of the most coveted individual honors in our creative community.

Where can I view my saved art?

Your saved artwork is available in your profile gallery. You can export your masterpieces in high resolution directly to your device or share them on social media.

How can I suggest a topic for the blog?

We'd love to hear from you! If you have a question about art history, techniques, or legendary creators, please use the contact form on our home page.
