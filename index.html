The Pillars of Digital Art

Understanding the dynamics of the online drawing world rests on knowing the tool structure, the community rules, and the road to the hall of fame.

Learning Path

Online drawing is the pinnacle of collaborative creativity, accessible to everyone.

  • Basics: Understanding pressure sensitivity and layers.
  • Community: Sharing and receiving feedback in real-time.
  • The Final: One match to crown the world champion.

Creative Scale

The art community is a marathon of consistency and creative pride.

  • Daily Tasks: A new drawing prompt every single day.
  • Global Users: Join millions of artists worldwide.
  • No Cost: High quality tools, completely free.

Key Metrics to Watch

Modern art games are driven by engagement. From stroke accuracy to color vibrancy, your metrics tell the story.

Artistic Accuracy

Awarded to the most precise recreation of a prompt. Perfecting your eye-to-hand coordination is the ultimate goal for every artist.

Mastering Layers

Just like the legends of art history, using layers effectively allows for complex compositions and non-destructive editing.

Community Rating

The number of likes and shares your artwork receives. Positive engagement often determines who climbs the hall of fame.
